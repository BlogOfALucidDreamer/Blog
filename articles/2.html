How to have lucid dreams ?

In this article, we will seeHow to have lucid dreams ? There are a lot of techniques and mindsets that will help you to have lucid dreams. In this article, we will see some of them, but we will explore those techniques and mindsets more in details in the future.

Keeping a dream journal

The very first step is to keep a dream journal. Keeping a dream journal will enable you to remember your dreams better because if you can't remember your dreams then, you will not be able to remember your lucid ones, so keeping a dream journal is very important and it's the first thing you need to do if you want to experience lucid dreams.
So take a little notebook you can acceed once you wake up and write your dreams when you have the best memory of you dream so at once you wake up. When you write your dream, remember to add as much as details as possible, and not just the images that comes to you, but your emotions, sensations, sounds you've heard, thought you had. Even if you can't remember anything, write in your journal that you have no memory about your dream of this night.
This dream journal will be very usefull because every night, just before you go to sleep, you will have to read your last dream.

The reality checks

A reality check is a second very important thing you need to learn before jumping into the techniques to become lucid. A reality check is a little reflex or something that allows you to know if you're in a dream or in reality, if you've seen the film Inception, it's like their totems but in more practicle and if you havn't seen it yet, Je vous le consielle fortement. Like I already said in my previous article, the dreams seems just like real life but there a few details that changes in dreams. That's why you need to learn what those details are.
There exists a lot of reality checks.

To know if you're in a dream or in reality, you can :

  • Look at your hands because in a dream they will look weird or you will not have more or less fingers
  • Boucher your nose and try to breath through it because in a dream it would works
  • Look at a text two times and see if it has changed
  • Look at a clock Two times and see if the time is the same
