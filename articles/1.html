What is a Lucid dream ?

First, What is a lucid dream ? A lucid dream is when you know that you are dreaming while the dream is still happening. Scientifically, it is an activation of your critical mind while you are still dreaming.

Your dreams seem very realistic while you're in them, but once you wake up, you remember the strange little details or the huge inconsistency of the dream. Your ability to have lucid dreams will depend on how critical you can be in a dream and how lucid you can stay in it, but guess what ? You can learn this mindset and you are in the right place !

There are a lot of techniques that we will explore that allow you to have lucid dreams. I'm not going to say that it will be easy or that you will have lucid dreams tonight, but if you really want to experience lucid dreams, or if you've been struggling with it for a while, then by following the learning steps correctly, you will probably have some lucid adventures within 1 to 2 months.

The incredible things you can do in lucid dreams !

If you're new here, you might not realise how incredible lucid dreams are and all of the incredible things you can do in a lucid dream !

When you become aware that you are dreaming, if you have enough lucidity, with some training, you will be able to control the dream !
By controlling the dream, I mean you can do everything you want, you can control the dream characters, control the scenario, control the dream physics, choose any place where you want your dream to take place, whether it exists or not, give yourself superpowers like flying or make objects levitate, stopping the dream time or teleporting. In a lucid dream, you can also face your fears with no consequences in real life !

It's a new world that opens up to you, it's your own world. You can control everything, it's like virtual reality, but you control the scenario in real time, you experience emotions and all your senses — touch, taste, sounds... You can teleport into the universe of your favorite film or video-game, or fly across an imaginary world that is entirely yours.

Do you want to know the best part about lucid dreams ? It feels just like real life !
Sometimes it even feels more real than real life itself because in a dream all of your emotions are amplified ten times !
